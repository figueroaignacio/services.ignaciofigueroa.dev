## Who is this for?

The Pro plan is for businesses that have outgrown a single landing page — or never had a proper website to begin with. It covers every page your company needs: who you are, what you do, who's behind it, and how to reach you.

It's the plan most of my clients end up choosing because it gives you everything that actually matters without paying for things you don't need.

## What's included — in plain terms

**Up to 8 pages.** That means home, services, about, team, contact, and a couple more if you need them. Everything is interconnected and consistent. No page looks out of place.

**Custom design.** I don't pick a theme and change your logo. Every page is designed from scratch to match your brand identity — colors, typography, spacing, and feel.

**Google-optimized from day one.** Technical SEO is built in: clean URLs, proper headings, meta tags, sitemaps, structured data. Your site will be indexable and competitive from launch.

**Content panel.** Need to update your team page, change a price, or add a new service? You'll have a simple interface to do that without touching any code.

**One integration.** This could be your CRM, your booking system, your analytics, your newsletter tool — whatever matters most to your workflow. I connect it.

## How the process works

1. **Discovery call** — We map out the full site: pages, goals, audience, content, and design direction.
2. **Content and copy** — You provide raw content; I help structure it. If you need copy written, we can discuss that separately.
3. **Design and build** — I work page by page. You review as we go, not just at the end.
4. **SEO and integrations** — Once the structure is solid, I layer in the technical optimizations and connect your tools.
5. **Launch** — Full deployment, domain setup, and a walkthrough of how to use the content panel.

## After launch

Post-launch support for fixes and adjustments is included for 14 days. After that, I offer optional maintenance packages if you'd like ongoing help.

This site is yours — all source code, all assets, full ownership.
