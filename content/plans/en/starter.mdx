## Who is this for?

The Starter plan is built for people who need a professional online presence without overcomplicating it. If you're a freelancer, a professional, or someone launching a new idea, this is your starting point.

It's not a template. It's not a drag-and-drop builder. It's a focused, custom-built page designed around your brand and your goals.

## What you actually get

A single, well-crafted page that does one thing: tell people who you are, what you do, and give them a reason to reach out.

The page is broken into up to 5 sections — typically a hero, an about or services block, a highlights section, and a contact form. Every section is designed to move visitors from curiosity to action.

**Design** is custom. Not from a library. Not a theme. It's built around your colors, your content, and how you want to be perceived.

**The contact form** is connected to your inbox. When someone fills it out, you get an email immediately. No third-party services required.

**Deployment** is handled for you. By the end of the project, your page is live, indexed, and ready to share.

## How the process works

1. **You brief me** — We have a short conversation (or you fill out a form) covering your goals, audience, references, and any copy you have.
2. **I design and build** — You'll see a working version, not mockups. Real code, real browser.
3. **You review and refine** — Up to two rounds of revisions included.
4. **It goes live** — I handle deployment and give you access to everything.

## What happens after launch?

The site is yours — all the code, all the assets. I don't lock you in. If you want to add pages later or upgrade to a full business site, you're already on the right foundation.

Post-launch support for small fixes is included for 7 days.
