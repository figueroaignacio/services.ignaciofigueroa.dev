## Sofia Caero Studio

A custom digital portfolio designed and developed to highlight the artistic direction, photography, and creative projects of Sofia Caero.

### Key features

- **Deliberate Typography**: Restructured typeface hierarchy emphasizing clarity and editorial flow.
- **Dynamic Projects Showcase**: Visual grids displaying artwork and imagery at full resolutions without sacrificing page performance.
- **Clean Interactions**: Micro-animations on interactive components ensuring user actions feel responsive and alive.
